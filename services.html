Tourist Services

Everything you need for a comfortable stay in Arrecife: accommodation, gastronomy, and transport.

Accommodation

Hotel Lancelot

Located near Playa del Reducto, this hotel offers comfortable rooms, a pool, and beautiful sea views. Perfect for being close to the beach.

Arrecife Gran Hotel & Spa

A luxury hotel with panoramic views, a spa, and fine dining. It is the tallest building in Lanzarote, ideal for a relaxing and exclusive stay.

Gastronomy & Restaurants

Arrecife offers a mix of traditional Canarian cuisine and international options.

Charco de San Ginés Area

The best place for dinner. A picturesque lagoon surrounded by terraces offering fresh fish, tapas, and local wines.

Marina Lanzarote

Modern restaurants located in the port area, perfect for a sophisticated meal overlooking the yachts.

Public Transport

Intercity Bus (Guaguas)

Arrecife is the central hub for the island's bus network. The main bus station (Intercambiador) connects the capital with Costa Teguise, Puerto del Carmen, and Playa Blanca.

  • Line 03: Arrecife - Puerto del Carmen
  • Line 01: Arrecife - Costa Teguise

Taxi Services

Taxis are white with a red stripe. They are available 24/7 at main stops or by calling +34 928 803 104.

Shops & Markets

Calle Real (Calle León y Castillo): The main pedestrian shopping street with boutiques, souvenirs, and cafes.

Saturday Market: Held every Saturday morning near San Ginés church, selling local crafts and agriculture.

Health & Emergency

Hospital José Molina Orosa: The main general hospital of Lanzarote, located on the outskirts of Arrecife.

Emergency Number: 112 (Available in multiple languages).
