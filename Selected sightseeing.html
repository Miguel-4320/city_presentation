Selected Sightseeing

Explore the must-visit landmarks of Arrecife and Lanzarote: from urban beaches to volcanic landscapes.

Playa del Reducto

The main urban beach of Arrecife. With its golden sand and calm waters, it's perfect for swimming, relaxing, or spending a day at the beach. Located just a short walk from the city center.

Activities & Services

  • Water Activities: Kayaking, windsurfing, and paddleboarding.
  • Dining: Café de la Playa, Restaurante El Charco nearby.
  • Comfort: Sunbed/umbrella rentals, restrooms, and showers.

Castillo de San José (MIAC)

A historic fortress offering panoramic views of the harbor. Today, it houses the International Museum of Contemporary Art (MIAC), blending history with modern creativity.

Visit Information

  • Opening Hours: Daily from 11:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
  • Entry Fee: €5.20 per adult (discounts for students).

Montañas del Fuego (Timanfaya)

Home to the "Mountains of Fire", a dramatic volcanic landscape formed in the 18th century. Witness geothermal demonstrations where the earth's heat ignites plants instantly.

Visit Information

  • Activities: Guided bus tour, geothermal demos, El Diablo Restaurant.
  • Opening Hours: Daily from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
  • Entry Fee: Approx. €22.00 per adult.
