Selected Sightseeing

Explore the must-visit landmarks of Arrecife and Lanzarote: from urban beaches to volcanic landscapes.

Playa del Reducto

The main urban beach of Arrecife. With its golden sand and calm waters, it's perfect for swimming, relaxing, or spending a day at the beach. Located just a short walk from the city center.

Activities & Services

  • Water Activities: Kayaking, windsurfing, and paddleboarding.
  • Dining: Café de la Playa, Restaurante El Charco nearby.
  • Comfort: Sunbed/umbrella rentals, restrooms, and showers.

Tips for Visiting

Make the most of your sightseeing experience in Lanzarote with these practical tips:

Save Money with Vouchers

If you plan to visit Timanfaya, Jameos del Agua, and Cueva de los Verdes, buy a CACT Multi-Centre Ticket (Bono) to save up to 20% on entrance fees.

Wear Comfortable Shoes

Most attractions involve walking on volcanic ground. Leave the flip-flops for the beach; sturdy sneakers are recommended for Timanfaya and the caves.

Rent a Car

While Arrecife has buses, the best way to explore the island's landmarks at your own pace is by renting a car. Parking is free at most major tourist sites.

Best Time to Visit

Popular spots like Timanfaya or Jameos del Agua get crowded around noon. Visit early in the morning (before 10:00) or late afternoon to avoid long queues.
